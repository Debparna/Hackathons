About Me

Hi there, my name is Debparna Pratiher and I am glad you found me.

I am a designer, photographer, aquatic fanatic and constant work-in-progress.

I focus in brand identity development to create simple and functional brands. My goal is to merge the brightness of personalities and the simplicity of modern design into something meaningful, useful and above all, fun. Design aside, find me swimming in the ocean, watching some feel good comedy shows, and excessively listening to my favorite artists, Odesza and Oh Wonder.
